🐍 Snake Tongue Phrases

Practical expressions in Ophidian

Everyday greetings

  • Hello! — (ge)ssi'i!
  • Hi! — ssee!
  • How are you? — śi (ge)ceșassa?
  • I'm fine — (ši)ceșassa
  • Goodbye — (ge)ceșassa!
  • Bye! — sse'e!
  • Later! — šala!

Introductions

  • What is your name? — (ge)sataxxa ssi'i?
  • My name is [N] — (ši)sataxxa [N]
  • Where are you from? — (ge)jišissa xaa gaśi ssi'i?
  • I'm from [N] — jišissa xaa [N]

Politeness and responses

  • Yes — hee
  • No — ssaa
  • Excuse me! — (ši)xessege
  • Sorry! — (ši)taaggaśiil (xaa ge)
  • Thank you! — hee(ge)seșesse

Speaking & Language

  • I speak — (ši)hissa
  • I don't speak — ssaa(ši)hissa
  • Do you speak? — śi (ge)hissa?
  • Do you speak English? — śi hissa heļļiș?
  • Russian — lus
  • German — cutis
  • French — laas
  • Serbian — šiji

Examples in context

Three yellow snakes attack a small bird:

secişi seteșșa selata sejahassi tešece tešiisa

I want to eat two eggs:

šeqeșesse śa-seșesse teja tettaa

O, hear the sound of this language!

heehašaxxa ši śahisa-ji haša!
