The Sound of the Serpent

Introduction

Snake Tongue, or Ophidian, has a phonetic system designed to echo the natural hissing, slithering, and whispering qualities of serpent speech. It favors sibilants, glottals, and uncommon fricatives, making it both evocative and challenging for human speakers.
