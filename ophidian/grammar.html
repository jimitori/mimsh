Agreement and Prefix Matching

In Ophidian, adjectives, numerals, and verbs agree with the nouns they describe or interact with. This agreement is marked by repeating the noun's class prefix in the related words.

This prefix matching helps clarify relationships between words, especially in sentences with multiple nouns. It is obligatory and applies consistently throughout the grammar.
