Content Writer App

Content Writer is a product designed to help SEO specialists generate SEO articles that rank in SERP for their clients' websites using artificial intelligence.

Goals

Design the product during its MVP stage and further integrate it into our existing SEO platform.

My Role

I was responsible for creating the entire product design and logic, ensuring that all stakeholder-requested features were well-placed, visually appealing, and user-friendly.

Duration

9 months

Process

Detailed explanation of your approach and methodology.

MVP Design Enhancement

Initially, I received the MVP and organized its structure. My role involved refining its design and logic, incorporating all necessary functions.

System Integration

I adapted our design system to integrate Content Writer with other products in our SEO platform. This required developing new design system components and ensuring coherence with existing elements.

User Flow Redesign

I revised the original user flow from the MVP, creating a new logic that better met the stakeholders' evolving ideas and requirements.

Documentation and Collaboration

I mapped all the produt functionality in Figjam to fully understand it and not to miss any states in the design process. I also used a git-like approach for page oraganization for the developers to always have the latest versions of the designs to base their work upon and not be distracted by ongoing iterations.
