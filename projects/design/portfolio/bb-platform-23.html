Brainbox.VC platform

Brainbox.VC is a crowd-investing platform that allows startups to receive funding from individual users registered as investors.

Goals

Develop a crowd-investing platform, ensuring it to be user-friendly and visually coherent. Design a scalable and simple branding guidelines and materials. Create pitch presentations that would bring investments into the project.

My Role

I created the platform design & logic from scratch in close collaboration with the stakeholders. I also developed marketing materials and pitch decks.

Duration

12 months

Process

Brand Identity Creation

Starting from an existing logo, I developed a simple yet scalable branding for Brainbox, using basic illustrations, colors, and recognizable styles to create a cohesive brand identity.

Platform Design

I designed the main landing page, the project listing page, and individual project pages with investment functionality.

User Personal Dashboard

I created a user dashboard where investors could manage their funds, view their investments, and access documents and calculations.

Design System Development

I built the initial version of the design system in close collaboration with frontend and backend developers.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Throughout the project, I worked directly with stakeholders, presenting designs, gathering feedback, and translating ideas into technical tasks. I also collaborated closely with frontend developers, discussing implementation and incorporating their suggestions for improvements.

Presentations for investors

I created several versions of the pitch deck of the platform which helped gain 20% of planned round of investments from big investors and other 80% via crowd-investing.
