Poster every day

Постер каждый день

Poster Every Day began as a skills-building challenge in graphic design but evolved into a creative playground where ideas, poems, quotes, and my world alphabet project freely intertwine.

Through this daily practice, I've developed my definitive style—a unique visual language that merges photography with graphic design elements and machine-generated randomness, creating a body of work where technical discipline and creative expression coexist.

Этот проект начался как попытка раскачать свои навыки графического дизайна, но постепенно он стал самостоятельной площадкой для реализации идей.
